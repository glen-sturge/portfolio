Bio

Hello! I'm Glen Sturge, a software development enthusiast from Newfoundland, Canada. Ever since I was young, I've been passionate about computers and technology, and have been the go-to person in my circles for tech-related solutions.

At the age of 40, I decided to pursue my passion and enrolled in the software development program at Keyin College in St. John's. Along the way, I've drawn inspiration from my diverse background in various roles, including flipping burgers, working in construction, providing tech support and customer service, working in security, and even being a janitor. These experiences have shaped my unique perspective on software development and enriched my problem-solving skills.

Beyond my career aspirations, my most cherished role in life is being a loving father to two amazing kids. As I complete my software development program, I look forward to leveraging my skills and experiences to create innovative solutions in the world of technology.
